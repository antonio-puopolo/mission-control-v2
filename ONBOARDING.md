# Mission Control - Quick Start Guide

Welcome to Mission Control, your AI-powered real estate operations hub.

## 🚀 Getting Started (2 minutes)

### Step 1: Access Your Dashboard
- URL: https://mission-control-v2-production-f25f.up.railway.app
- Bookmark this page for quick access
- Works on desktop, tablet, or mobile

### Step 2: First Time Setup
1. **LAP Tracker** - Start here
   - Click the "LAP Tracker" tab
   - Add your first LAP using the "+ New LAP" button
   - Fill in: Address, Client Name, Follow-up Date
   - Click "Create" to save

2. **Pipeline Organization**
   - LAPs auto-sort into pipelines based on timing:
     - 🔥 Pipeline A: Hot leads (1-3 months)
     - 📋 Pipeline B: Warm leads (3-6 months)  
     - 🕐 Pipeline C: Future leads (6+ months)
   - Drag cards between pipelines to reorganize

3. **Daily Workflow**
   - Red badges = Overdue follow-ups (action today!)
   - Yellow badges = Due soon (next 2 days)
   - Click any LAP card to expand details
   - Update notes after each interaction

## 📱 Mobile Tips
- Swipe left/right between pipeline columns
- Tap cards to expand/collapse
- Pull down to refresh data
